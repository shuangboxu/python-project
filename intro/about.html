Movie Recommendation Project Overview 电影推荐系统项目简介

We build a multi-dimensional recommendation system around TMDB movie data, combining content attributes, user popularity, business performance, and temporal plus regional factors to deliver film lists that balance diverse preferences. The project emphasizes data traceability and metric interpretability so that the recommendations stay trustworthy and easy to explain. 我们围绕 TMDB 电影数据构建多维推荐系统，结合内容特征、用户热度、商业表现以及时间与地域因素，生成兼顾多元偏好的电影清单。项目强调数据可追溯性与指标解释性，让推荐结果既可靠又易于理解。

The project follows three stages: first, four members deliver component score tables for their respective feature modules; second, merge scripts complete normalization, weighted aggregation, and result review; third, outputs turn into webpages and presentation materials that showcase the logic and highlights to stakeholders. 项目划分为三个阶段：第一阶段四名成员按特征模块分别产出组件得分表；第二阶段通过合并脚本完成归一化、加权汇总与结果复核；第三阶段将成果转化为网页和汇报材料，向业务方展示推荐逻辑与亮点。

Team Roles 团队分工

  • Xu Shuangbo (Content) 许双博（Content）: Focuses on text features such as genres, keywords, overview, and title; delivers reports/tables/01_content_scores.csv; and maintains the related analysis scripts and logs. 聚焦 genres、keywords、overview、title 等文本特征，输出 reports/tables/01_content_scores.csv，并维护对应分析脚本与日志。
  • Wang Yunjia (Ratings & Popularity) 王韵嘉（Ratings & Popularity）: Evaluates rating heat using vote_average, vote_count, and popularity, designs the normalization scheme, and produces reports/tables/02_rating_scores.csv. 基于 vote_average、vote_count、popularity 评估评分热度，负责归一化方案设计，产出 reports/tables/02_rating_scores.csv。
  • Wu Hongwei (Business) 武红玮（Business）: Examines commercial value across budget, revenue, and production_companies, assesses ROI risks, and generates reports/tables/03_business_scores.csv. 分析 budget、revenue、production_companies 的商业价值，评估 ROI 风险，生成 reports/tables/03_business_scores.csv。
  • Li Shangze (Time & Locale) 李尚泽（Time & Locale）: Organizes temporal and regional signals such as release_date, runtime, spoken_languages, and original_language to provide scenario insights, delivering reports/tables/04_time_scores.csv. 梳理 release_date、runtime、spoken_languages、original_language 等时间与地域特征，为推荐结果提供场景洞察并输出 reports/tables/04_time_scores.csv。

The four component score sheets converge into reports/tables/final_recommendations.csv, and the visual page plus presentation materials clarify weights, normalization steps, and the standout recommendations. 四份组件得分将汇聚为 reports/tables/final_recommendations.csv，并在可视化页面与汇报材料中讲清权重、归一化过程与推荐亮点。
