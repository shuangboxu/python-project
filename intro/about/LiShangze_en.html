Alan·R·Lee

CIO/CTO

Responsibilities

Technical lead for the movie recommendation project. I design the data architecture, maintain the ETL pipelines that feed every scoring module, and review merge scripts before they reach production dashboards. When something breaks, I am usually the person on call to trace the lineage and document the fix.

Interests & Research

Fascinated by film metadata standards, knowledge graphs, and how critic text can be transformed into structured features. I spend a lot of time experimenting with reproducible analytics stacks, benchmarking Spark versus DuckDB, and curating open datasets on streaming availability so the team can cross-check our recommendations.

Platform Practice

Built monitoring notebooks that surface pipeline latency and schema drift in near real time. Partner closely with each scorer to productionise their models, containerise dependencies, and package CLI tools so the rest of the cohort can run them with a single command. Outside of work I still collect theatre programmes—some habits never change.

Message to Team

Reliable data is the best teammate. Document everything, keep the logs tidy, and we will have a recommendation stack that survives demo day.
