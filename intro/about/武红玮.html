Wu Hongwei 武红玮

User Research · Project Journal 用户研究 · 项目记录

Profile 基本概况

First-year student in Data Science and Big Data Technology who enjoys discovering stories in the details. Responsible for user insights and the project journal in the movie recommendation project, serving as the team’s recorder and listener.

数据科学与大数据技术专业大一学生，偏爱从细节里发现故事。负责电影推荐项目的用户洞察与项目日志，是团队的“记录者”与“倾听者”。

Project Role 项目角色

Organises questionnaires and interviews to summarise viewing preferences, acceptable recommendation frequency, and presentation styles, turning the “voice of users” into a requirements checklist. Also maintains reports/logs/ to record each scoring iteration, merge strategy, and issue list so the team can review the history at any time.

一方面组织问卷与访谈，归纳观影偏好、可接受的推荐频次和呈现方式，把“用户的声音”整理成需求清单；另一方面维护 `reports/logs/`，记录每次评分迭代、合并策略和问题清单，让团队随时回溯。

Interests & Exploration 兴趣与探索

Enjoys reading film reviews and long posts on Douban, and often turns daily viewing experiences into short essays. Curious about information architecture and web layouts, and currently learning how Markdown plus Obsidian can build a knowledge base to preserve user-research methods.

喜欢阅读影评、豆瓣长帖，也会把日常观影体验写成短文。对信息架构和网页排版充满好奇，正在学习如何用 Markdown + Obsidian 构建知识库，把用户研究方法沉淀下来。

Looking Ahead 未来期待

Hopes to bring the recommendation system closer to real audiences: when users see a suggestion, they feel “this suits me.” Next plans to feed research insights back into feature engineering so that a sense of warmth can also become a data point.

希望让推荐系统更贴近真实观众：当用户看到推荐时，会觉得“这正好符合我”。接下来计划把调研洞察回流到特征工程里，让“温度”也能成为数据点。
